Challenges in Teaching Data Science

  • Students are scared, not curious about data
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is crucial
  • Bad Assumptions can lead to bad statistics

Solutions

  • Interactive Workshops
    • EDA Workshop
  • Open Education Resources
    • R Bootcamp
  • Peer Mentoring and Teaching
    • BioData Club

Solution: R-Bootcamp

  • Self directed MOOC in learning statistical programming
    • Freely available
    • Self directed with drop-in sessions
  • Problem Set Driven
    • Basics of Data Wrangling/Visualization/Databases
  • Over 180 members have enrolled from OHSU
    • Students contribute feedback/shared authorship

https://github.com/laderast/r-bootcamp

Solution: Peer Mentoring and Teaching

  • BioData Club at OHSU
    • Foster a peer mentoring culture for students
    • Open to Everyone

    • Student led workshops and discussions
      • Experience-Based: What worked? What didnt?
      • Encourage life-long learning
    • Coworking: work on data together
